Design Principle: Hick’s Law

Little Caesars

The Rule of Hick’s Law increase the number of choices that we can make in this case food. The principle says that when more choices you give, the easier it is to choose nothing, because in this case the user has more difficulty. Basically, web designers make their sites the easier they can for understanding.
